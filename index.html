Educator Screens in Schools Survey

A survey for Pennsylvania K–12 educators

Section 1 of 5

Welcome!

PA Unplugged is conducting an Educator Screens in Schools Survey open to all Pennsylvania K–12 educators in public, private, and charter schools. We are gathering feedback about your experiences using technology in your classroom and across the school. The goal of this survey is to gain a clearer picture of how teachers are thinking about these important issues. The survey takes about 10 minutes to complete.

You can review the parent/caregiver companion survey results at paunplugged.org/dashboard.

Your input will help inform local and statewide advocacy efforts and your responses will help us build an anonymized public dashboard where you can explore and compare feedback across school districts throughout the state. To protect your privacy, demographic data visible on the dashboard will be shared only in aggregate, broken down by school district and grade level categories (K–2, 3–5, 6–8, and 9–12). The dashboard will be made public when we reach 500 responses. Please share with your colleagues.

As a thank you for your time, all respondents who provide an email address at the end of the survey will be entered into a raffle to win an Amazon gift card.

Please complete this survey by July 15, 2026. If you have questions, reach out at hello@paunplugged.org.
